FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest score in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers - and indeed in the history of international football - was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0. This legendary match also brought global renown for Archive Thomson, whose 13-goal haul set a new world record, wich stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.
